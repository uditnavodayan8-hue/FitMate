Rental Policy

Transparent terms and conditions for a smooth rental experience.

1. Booking Process

1.1 How to Book: You can book outfits through WhatsApp, by visiting our showroom, or by contacting us via phone/email. To confirm your booking, a security deposit and advance payment are required.

1.2 Availability: All rentals are subject to availability. We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for weddings and 1 week for party wear to ensure your preferred outfit is available.

1.3 Trial Appointments: We offer free trial appointments at our Ahmedabad showroom. You can try multiple outfits and finalize the one that fits perfectly. Trials must be booked in advance via WhatsApp.

2. Pricing & Payments

2.1 Rental Fees: All rental prices displayed on our website and showroom are for a standard 3-day rental period (collect 2 days before event, return 1 day after event).

2.2 Security Deposit: A refundable security deposit (typically 20-30% of the outfit value) is required at the time of booking. This deposit will be fully refunded upon safe return of the outfit in good condition.

2.3 Advance Payment: 50% of the rental fee is required as advance payment to confirm your booking. The remaining 50% must be paid at the time of outfit collection.

2.4 Payment Methods: We accept cash, UPI, bank transfer, and credit/debit cards.

2.5 No Hidden Charges: The prices quoted include the outfit and standard accessories as mentioned. Alterations, extended rental periods, and add-on accessories may incur additional charges, which will be communicated upfront.

4. Collection & Return

4.1 Collection: You can collect the outfit from our showroom during business hours (10 AM - 8 PM). Doorstep delivery within Ahmedabad is available for an additional fee.

4.2 Return: Outfits must be returned to our showroom within the rental period. We also offer doorstep pickup services for a fee.

4.3 Condition Upon Return: Outfits should be returned in the same condition as received. Normal wear and tear (minor creases, light makeup stains) are acceptable and will not be charged. Significant damage will be assessed as per our damage policy.

5. Alterations & Fitting

5.1 Free Minor Alterations: We provide free minor alterations (small adjustments to blouse, sleeves, length) to ensure perfect fit.

5.2 Major Alterations: Major alterations that significantly change the outfit structure may incur additional charges or may not be possible. Please consult with our team during trials.

5.3 Fitting Guarantee: If the outfit doesn't fit properly even after alterations, you can exchange it for another piece from our collection (subject to availability).

6. Cancellation Policy

6.1 Customer Cancellation:

  • Cancellation more than 15 days before event: Full refund of advance payment
  • Cancellation 8-15 days before event: 50% refund of advance payment
  • Cancellation less than 7 days before event: No refund

6.2 FitMate Cancellation: In rare cases where we cannot fulfill your booking due to unforeseen circumstances, we will offer an alternative outfit of equal or higher value or provide a full refund.

7. Hygiene & Safety

7.1 Cleaning Process: Every outfit is professionally dry-cleaned, sanitized with hospital-grade equipment, steam-ironed, and quality-checked before and after every rental.

7.2 Your Safety: We maintain the highest hygiene standards to ensure your health and safety. If you have any concerns about an outfit's cleanliness, please inform us immediately.

8. Customer Responsibilities

As a customer, you agree to:

  • Treat the rented outfit with care
  • Not sub-rent or lend the outfit to others
  • Avoid excessive makeup, perfume, or food stains
  • Store the outfit in the protective garment bag provided
  • Return the outfit on time and in good condition
  • Inform us immediately of any damage or accidents

9. Liability

FitMate Rentals is not liable for any allergic reactions, skin issues, or health problems arising from wearing rented outfits. While we maintain the highest hygiene standards, individual sensitivities vary, and customers rent at their own discretion.

10. Disputes & Resolution

In case of any disputes or disagreements, we encourage open communication. Please contact us via WhatsApp or visit our showroom to discuss the issue. We are committed to fair and amicable resolution of all concerns.
