Damage & Refund Policy

We believe in complete transparency. Our policy is designed to protect both lenders and borrowers while ensuring the highest quality standards for every rental.

1. Normal Wear and Tear

We expect items to be returned in good condition, but we understand that minor signs of use can occur. Normal wear and tear includes:

  • Minor loose threads or beads
  • Slight scuffing on the bottom hem
  • Easily removable faint marks
