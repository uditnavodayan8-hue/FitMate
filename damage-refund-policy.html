Damage & Refund Policy

Fair and transparent guidelines for damage assessment and security deposit refunds.

1. Understanding Normal Wear & Tear

At FitMate Rentals, we understand that outfits are meant to be worn and enjoyed. We do not charge for normal wear and tear that occurs during regular use at events. The following are considered acceptable and NOT chargeable:

  • Minor creases and wrinkles from wearing
  • Light makeup stains (foundation, lipstick on collar)
  • Small perspiration marks
  • Minor dust or dirt that can be dry-cleaned
  • Loose threads (if not pulled extensively)
  • Slight stretching of fabric from normal movement

These are expected and accounted for in our cleaning process. Your security deposit will NOT be affected by these issues.

2. Damage Categories & Charges

Damages beyond normal wear and tear are classified into three categories. Here is a quick summary:

Detailed breakdown of categories:

Category A: Minor Damage (10-25% of Security Deposit)

  • Removable stains (oil, food, wine) that require special treatment
  • Multiple loose embellishments (sequins, beads) that need re-stitching
  • Small tears (less than 2 inches) that can be repaired invisibly
  • Missing small accessories (pins, brooches) that can be replaced

Category B: Moderate Damage (30-60% of Security Deposit)

  • Permanent stains (bleach, ink, turmeric) that cannot be fully removed
  • Medium tears (2-5 inches) requiring visible repair work
  • Burn marks or holes from cigarettes, candles, etc.
  • Significant loss of embellishments affecting the design
  • Broken zippers or buttons requiring replacement

Category C: Major Damage (70-100% of Security Deposit + Additional Charges)

  • Large tears or rips (more than 5 inches) that cannot be repaired invisibly
  • Extensive permanent staining covering large areas
  • Lost or severely damaged main outfit pieces (dupatta, blouse, jacket)
  • Damage to the structural integrity of the outfit making it un-rentable
  • Alterations done by customer without permission

Note: For Category C damages that render the outfit un-rentable, additional charges (up to the full replacement value) may apply beyond the security deposit.

3. Damage Assessment Process

Step 1: Inspection Upon Return
When you return the outfit, our team will conduct a thorough inspection in your presence (if returning to showroom). We will note any damage and discuss it with you immediately.

Step 2: Professional Evaluation
Our cleaning and repair team will assess the damage category and repairability. This evaluation typically takes 1-2 business days.

Step 3: Communication
We will contact you via WhatsApp with photos of the damage, the category it falls under, and the deduction amount from your security deposit.

Step 4: Resolution
If you agree with the assessment, we proceed with the deduction. If you disagree, we can arrange a meeting to discuss and reach a fair resolution.

4. Security Deposit Refund Process

4.1 Timeline: Security deposits are refunded within 5-7 business days after outfit return and damage assessment.

4.2 Refund Method: Refunds are processed via the same payment method used for the deposit (UPI, bank transfer, etc.).

4.3 Full Refund: If the outfit is returned in good condition with only normal wear and tear, you receive 100% of your security deposit back.

4.4 Partial Refund: If damage is assessed, the applicable deduction is made, and the remaining amount is refunded.

4.5 No Refund: In rare cases of extremely severe damage (Category C), the full security deposit may be retained, and additional charges may apply.

5. Rental Fee Refunds

5.1 Cancellation Refunds: Rental fee refunds for cancellations are governed by our cancellation policy (see Rental Policy page).

5.2 Defective Outfit: If you receive a defective or damaged outfit from our end, you are entitled to:

  • Immediate replacement with another outfit of equal or higher value
  • OR Full refund of rental fee and security deposit
  • This must be reported within 24 hours of collection

5.3 Service Failure: If we fail to deliver your outfit on time or cannot fulfill your booking, you receive a full refund of all payments + a 20% compensation voucher for future rentals.

6. Insurance & Protection Options

6.1 Damage Protection Plan (Optional): For an additional 10% of the rental fee, you can opt for our Damage Protection Plan which covers all minor and moderate damages (Category A and B). Only major damages (Category C) would result in deductions.

6.2 How It Works: Inform us at the time of booking that you want to add Damage Protection. Pay the additional fee. If damage occurs, your security deposit is fully protected for Category A and B damages.

7. Customer Tips to Avoid Damage

  • Apply makeup and perfume before wearing the outfit
  • Avoid eating messy foods while wearing the outfit (especially red curries, turmeric-based dishes)
  • Store the outfit in the provided garment bag when not wearing it
  • Keep away from candles, diyas, and smoking areas
  • Handle embellishments and zari work gently
  • Transport the outfit carefully to avoid crushing or creasing
  • If any accident occurs (spill, tear), inform us immediately ‚Äî quick response can often minimize damage

8. Disputes & Fair Resolution

We believe in fair and transparent dealings. If you feel a damage assessment is unfair:

  • Request a detailed explanation with photos and evidence
  • Visit our showroom to inspect the damage yourself
  • We can seek a third-party expert opinion if needed
  • Our goal is always amicable resolution, not conflict

We value long-term relationships over short-term profits. If you're a reasonable and honest customer, we'll always work with you to find a fair solution.
